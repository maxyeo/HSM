    Frequenty Asked Questions

    What do I need to do before my pick-up appointment?

    The following packing guidelines will help ensure that your items are not harmed over the summer. Wrap all items with bubble wrap or paper so that they fit snugly inside your boxes. Label all items on ALL SIDES We will be stacking your boxes so fill them up and use adequate cushioning material. Tape drawers shut if you plan on storing plastic drawers. Make sure to defrost and dry your refrigerator prior to your pick-up appointment. Do not pack any liquids! If you are storing microwaves or toasters, make sure to tape their cords to the box. Wrap any loose items that you plan on storing. Seal your boxes! Makes sure to tape both the top and bottom of your boxes. Make sure that your boxes do not exceed 70lbs. We will charge you an extra $10 per box that weighs more than 70lbs. A $5 service charge will apply to any item that has not been packaged by these guidelines.

    How do I schedule a pick-up appointment?

    You can schedule a pick-up appointment through our online scheduling system.

    Do I need to meet the movers anywhere? What do I do on the day of my pick-up appointment?

    Our movers will come right to your room. All you need to do before your pick-up appointment is make sure that all of your items are packed up.
    If you do not answer the door, we will call you at the phone number you provide when you schedule your appointment.
    If you miss your appointment we will try our best to accommodate a rescheduling.
    Appointments cancelled within 5 days of the appointment date will be charged a cancellation fee of $20.

    How do I pay?

    You can pay immediately after your pick-up appointment with either a credit card or a check. The check should be made out to Hopkins Student Movers. We do not accept cash.

    When will I get my stuff back?

    We will e-mail you once we finalize our move in schedule. You will then be able to schedule a redelivery appointment the same way you scheduled your pick-up appointment.
